About Me
Hi there! My name is Amy (she/they), and I am a Data Science and Public Health major at UC Berkeley graduating in December 2022. I am passionate about the intersection of technology and social impact.
Last summer, I interned at Grainite as a Software Engineering Intern focused on building back-end database tools in C++. Outside of work, you can find me hiking and camping in the beautiful outdoors of Northern California, climbing at Berkeley Ironworks, or playing board games with my friends.
